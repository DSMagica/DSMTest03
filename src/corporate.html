Corporate Profile

Building and Civil Construction Services

Kehew Builders is a Western Canadian general contracting construction company with a unique perspective on the construction industry. We provide services in the residential, commercial, light and heavy industrial, energy, seniors housing, and civil construction sectors.

With over 40 years of industry experience, hundreds of residential units, and over $100 million in value, our owner Nick Matichuk has developed a deep understanding of the marketplace and its customers.

  • Small and Agile: Our small, single-owner model and extensive network of professionals allow us to deploy resources effectively on any project.
  • Relationship-Based: We value discussion, participation, customization, and transparency with our clients.
  • Customer Focused: We work hard to provide an outstanding product at a competitive price through value-based construction.

The Kehew Construction Process

Pre-Contract Stage

Before a contract is written, we meet with potential clients to discuss objectives, plans, and requirements. We help with financing and review construction needs—from parking and garage bays to site layout and renderings—ensuring you get an optimal building design.

Contract Stage

In this stage, we finalize the key terms. Whether you choose a Design-Build model, Construction Management, or a traditional Design-Bid-Build method, our goal is to add value and secure a Firm Fixed Price by assuming full responsibility for cost overruns.

Health & Safety

Our commitment extends to ensuring a safe worksite for our employees, subcontractors, and the public. We maintain high standards for site safety, enforce safe work procedures, and continuously strive for an injury-free workplace.
